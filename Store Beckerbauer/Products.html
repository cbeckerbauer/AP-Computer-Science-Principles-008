About Us:

Creel's Flowers was formed in 1943 by Creelisel Thwaite in Ireland. She was inspired by her friends from her youth noteably dragons. They all loved the natrual beauty of the world, especially flowers. Theoradus was the most fond of nature's flowers especially Snapdragons. When he passed on, Creelisel was very moved and in his memory started this shop, with the first flower being sold was Snapdragons from Theoradus's garden. Since we first opened, we have grown to be semi-international with branches all over Europe, and America. Creelisel passed away in 2014, but her business still survives very much to this day. The business is currently in her daughter's hands and we foresee much growth and propsarity of this company.
